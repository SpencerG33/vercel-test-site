ABOUT THE LEAGUE

THE TAPWATR DYNASTY

What started as twelve guys who probably spend too much time thinking about football has evolved into one of the most competitive dynasty leagues you've never heard of. Tapwatr Dynasty is a 12-team, 4-division monster with deep rosters, taxi squads, and enough trade drama to keep five writers busy year-round.

Dynasty means you keep your players. It also means you live with your mistakes. That running back you drafted three years ago who hasn't scored since? Still on your roster. This league has a long memory.

Coverage is powered by five distinct voices who bring radically different perspectives on the same twelve rosters. Agree with none of them. Trust all the data.
